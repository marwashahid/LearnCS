What we do

Let's Learn CS is a platform that offers one-to-one classes to students of O Levels, A Levels, IGCSE, Matric, Intermediate as well as to young students who aim to delve into technology at a younger age. It means that our classes are live on zoom so we only take a limited number of students at a time. The aim of this platform is to provide affordable tutoring option for students.

All our classes are live on zoom or Microsoft teams. This allows the students to interact with the teacher and ask questions and get feedback.
